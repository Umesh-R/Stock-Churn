Stock Churn Advisor

Stock Churn Advisor is a webapp to help find out how an investor can distribute their funds in a stock portfolio with fixed funds to invest. This will help calculate the risk and returns of various possible portfolios and it gives the investor the portfolio with maximum returns for the risk taken, portfolio with the minimum risk and also allows the user to choose a portfolio based on their risk appetite.
